This page provides a comprehensive reference of available audit log services and events. By understanding which events are logged in the audit logs, your enterprise can monitor detailed Databricks usage patterns in your account.

To access and query your account's audit logs, use the [audit log system table (Public Preview)](/aws/en/admin/system-tables/audit-logs). Databricks highly discourages you from moving this data outside the platform because it can expose sensitive data and put your deployment at risk. Due to the nature of audit log contents, you are reminded that you are responsible for maintaining the security and preventing misuse of any exported audit logs.

## Audit log considerations

* The services in this reference are organized by `audit_level`, with workspace-level actions presented first and account-level actions following.
* Because account-level actions aren't specific to a single workspace, the `workspace_id` in account-level logs is recorded as `0`.
* Most audit logs are only accessible from the region in which they are recorded.
